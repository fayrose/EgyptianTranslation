Transliteration and translation for &quot;Magical spell for the
protection of a baby&quot;, following De Buck (1948), p. 115. The
positions are added on the basis of Hieratische Papyrus (1911), p.
XVII. Not considered was the transcription by Sethe (1924), pp. 51-52.

For comments on the text see Sethe (1959), pp. 76-77.

The transliteration throughout follows Hannig (1995).

###

A. De Buck. <i>Egyptian Readingbook</i>. Ares Publishers, Chicago,
Illinois, 1948.

R. Hannig. <i>Grosses Handwörterbuch Ägyptisch-Deutsch: die Sprache
der Pharaonen (2800-950 v.Chr.)</i>. Verlag Philipp von Zabern, 1995.

<i>Hieratische Papyrus aus den königlichen Museen zu Berlin</i>. J.C.
Hinrichs'sche Buchhandlung, Leipzig, 1911.

K. Sethe. <i>Aegyptische Lesestücke</i>. J.C. Hinrichs'sche
Buchhandlung, Leipzig, 1924.

K. Sethe. <i>Erläuterungen zu den Ägyptischen Lesestücken</i>. Georg
Olms Verlagsbuchhandlung, Hildesheim, 1959.
