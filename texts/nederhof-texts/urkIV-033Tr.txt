Transliteration and translation of four small inscriptions near
Tombos, describing Tuthmosis I as destroyer of the Nubians,
following the transcription of Sethe (1927), number 33 (pp. 87-88).

For published translations, see Sethe (1914), number 33 (pp. 44-45).
